# Market Intelligence & Competitor Framework

## Intelligence Gathering Model
1. **Financial Discovery**: SEC Edgar (10-K, 10-Q), Yahoo Finance, Crunchbase.
2. **Product Audit**: Website analysis, product docs, roadmaps, pricing models.
3. **Strategic Moves**: Press releases, leadership changes, M&A activity.
4. **Digital Presence**: SEO strategy, social sentiment, glassdoor (employee sentiment).

## Competitive Radar Dimensions
- **Value Prop**: What is their core differentiator?
- **Moat**: Patents, proprietary tech, network effects.
- **Vulnerabilities**: Tech debt, high churn, dependency risks.
- **Expansion**: Hiring patterns (LinkedIn/Indeed), new office locations.

## SWOT Assessment Matrix
| Category | External/Internal | Focal Points |
|----------|-------------------|--------------|
| **Strengths** | Internal | Financial health, proprietary IP, brand equity. |
| **Weaknesses**| Internal | Operational silos, legacy tech, churn rates. |
| **Opportunities** | External | Unmet market needs, regulatory shifts, M&A. |
| **Threats** | External | Disruptive tech, new entrants, economic shifts. |

## Executive Briefing Structure
- **Key Findings**: 3-5 high-impact market shifts.
- **Strategic Implications**: How this affects "Us".
- **Action Plan**: Immediate vs Long-term defensive/offensive moves.
- **Priority**: High/Medium/Low urgency.
